  • About Me

    Hi. My name is Stanislaw Bogatkin and I'm IT guy.

    From about middle 2014 I'm working on OpenStack project, named Fuel. It's a full-fledged control plane for OpenStack installation and it's absolutely free and opensource, so if you interested in easy OpenStack install, you definitely should try it. Between my working sessions I prefer to develop a little things to get people life easier, mostly on Python3. My preferrable operating system for everyday is Arch Linux.

    Apart from work and IT, I like athletics, boxing, bikes and travelling. Also I have a little collection of knives (but it constantly grows, cause I really like wonderful knives and usually cannot overcome the temptation to buy another one).
